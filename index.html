My Dream

I dream of building a society of dancers in the valley of nature. It’s a place where everything has a soul in dance. A space where you breathe, eat, sleep, dream, talk and walk through dance. Our class room is a place where dancers drop negative qualities such as jealousy, small-mindedness, greed and malice to embrace their colleagues as sisters and support each other in their journey towards becoming dancers of merit

About Us

In 2000, Kalamandalam Anjali Sunil founded a school for Classical Bharatanatyam, dance in Ernakulam located at Kothamangalam and the institute is affiliated to kerala sangeetha nadaka academy.She trains 300 students in Bharatanatyam, Mohini Attam and other forms of dances. Her students are participating in the CBSE State school kalotsavam & Kerala state School kalotsavam.

About School

Students receive sound training in the practice and theory of several classical dance styles from India namely Bharathanatyam,Kuchipudi and Mohiniattam.Students also receive training in semi- classical and folk dancing.Natyanjali is dedicated to promoting Indian dance in keeping with the strict traditions of these ancient arts.The mission of our institution is to provide dance training of the highest quality while keeping tuition costs low thereby enabling all interested children and adults to learn these ancient art forms from india.

About Teacher

Kalamandalam Anjali Sunil  as a dance teacher for the last fifteen years harmonize the victory of buds in the temple “ Natyanjali”. is able to update and rejuvenate her knowledge, which is very essential in any teaching activity. One of the distinct features of Kalamandalam Anjali Sunil’s teaching method is the creation of an intensive learning environment where the students can closely interact, meditate, encourage and discipline each other, discovering their individual interpretations and performing styles instead of blindly copying some "standard" dancer's movements and manners. The emphasis is to invoke the natural talent of the individual and urge to bring out the innate creative ability.
